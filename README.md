For my final project in CIM 423, Building Virtual Worlds, I decided to create an informative experience about the history of house music, or more specifically, the story of The Warehouse in Chicago.  First, the player starts outside a nightclub called The Warehouse where they interact with a bouncer.  From there, they interact with people inside the club to hear their thoughts on the Warehouse and what it means to them.
